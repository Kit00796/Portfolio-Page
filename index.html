Projects

This website is "I need a break". It started as just a pahe to practiceand sharpen my skills, but it has evolved and continues to be a passion project for me. I've had the great opportunity talking with many programmers (whether though forums or in person) and they all lent me the same bit of advice: "Know when to take a break!".

The idea for this project was to create a space where programmers can come to unwind. It would include games and puzzles that had a programming theme as well as its own dedicated forum area where people could connect with each other. Another sectiton would be dedicated to reading material, whether it be "what's going on in the world of tech" or a maybe a few sections of things that are as far away from tech and programming as they could be.

This project hold a special place for me and I have very big plans for it as it's something that not only I may benefit from, but something that thousands may find helpful.

This webpage is titled "Silver Fins". This project was my first "built-from-scratch" websites. It is a mock page for a swim club that focuses on swimming the lakes of New England.

I've been a swimming fanatic for my entire life, so I made this page as something that I would hope to find. I wanted to keep the layout as a simple, singular page as those who are looking for a club may not want to click 6 different nav items just to find days/times/locations.

With this project I wanted to do something more practical; something that could be used by anyone or everyone who has no idea what they want to cook.

This meal generator has a variety of food categories and styles. It's designed to be able to come up with a random mix and combined will inspire someone to create something (hopefully delicious).
